A land of warriors with serene and virgin beaches of Malvan
welcomes you to its shores

Often less familiar places conceal severe beauty and Malvan is one precious jewel. Malvan is a dense city located on the coast of Western India and boasts some lovely beaches.

Literally, complete with live coral reefs, brave stories of pirates and warriors and undiscovered beaches. It’s a land still protected by the historicism of Chhatrapati Shivaji. The tiny villages and cities of Malvan taluka live in the shadows of the great fighter both happy and consummated. This place is more of a quest on contrary to revisiting an earlier pre-existing journey.

Several fictional and some more credible stories associated with the name Malvan exist. One of them is that salt producers used the term “Mahalavan” to explain the vicinity wealthy in salt. It was described as a compound word from “Maha” which means enormous and “lavan” that means plantation (or salt). Another possibility may be a phonetic spinoff of the compound of “Mad” and “Ban”. Malvani for coconut trees (Mad) and garden (Ban) with reference to the massive range of coconut trees within the region.

Here at Malvan Beach House, amidst coconut tree horizons and the melodious sound of waves get an escape from the daily madness. One can also just relax and enjoy the view all day long.
